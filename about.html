A little about me

I’m a software developer and a storyteller. I’m constantly looking for new ways to tell stories that matter in new ways.

Most recently, my projects include a better way to explore all of the our data collected about us as we move through life.

Although I’m a Minnesota native, my passion for journalism drew me southward to study at the Missouri School of Journalism. In the spring of 2013, I graduated magna cum laude with majors in Journalism and Spanish.

During my four years in college, I worked as a producer for Minnesota Public Radio, a videographer for PBS NewsHour’s Inaug Blog at President Obama’s second inauguration in Washington, D.C., and as a multimedia intern for el Foro de Periodismo Argentino in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I graduated from the Missouri School of Journalism’s Convergence Program, which focuses on finding the best type of media for each story.
